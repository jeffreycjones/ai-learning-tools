AI Tools & Integration

Approved and pilot AI tools available for use in course design, content development, and learning delivery. Each tool has been evaluated for data privacy, accessibility, and instructional alignment.

Approved & Pilot Tools

🏫
TAMU AI Chat (BETA)

Texas A&M's secure, institutionally hosted AI platform at tamu.ai. Provides access to multiple models (GPT-4, Claude Sonnet, Gemini) through a single interface. Sessions are not used to train external models. Supports multi-model comparison and merged outputs.

Approved — Institutional
🎨
Microsoft Copilot

In-context assistance within Word, PowerPoint, and Teams for document drafting and slide creation.

Approved
📊
Grammarly Business

Consistency editing, tone calibration, and accessibility checks across all course-facing content.

Approved
📚
NotebookLM (Google)

Source-grounded Q&A for research synthesis. Faculty can upload readings and generate study guides for students.

Approved
📷
Articulate Rise + AI Assist

Cloud-based course authoring platform for building responsive, interactive online lessons. AI Assist generates knowledge checks, suggests content layouts, and drafts lesson blocks from learning objectives. Used by TLI designers for Flex Online module development and faculty self-service course builds.

Pilot
