Tips for Effective FAQs

Proactive Communication Reduces Email

Comprehensive FAQs address common student questions before they ask, reducing email volume and improving student satisfaction.

Update After Each Semester

Keep a running list of questions students actually ask. Use this list to update your FAQs each semester to stay relevant.

Include Technology Troubleshooting

Canvas and Zoom issues are common. Include clear, step-by-step troubleshooting guidance or links to technical support.

Address AI Policy Directly

Students are uncertain about AI use in academic work. Be explicit about what is and is not permitted in your course.

Keep Answers Short and Actionable

FAQs should be scannable. Use concise language and tell students exactly what to do (e.g., "submit through Canvas by 11:59 PM").

Maintain Consistency with Syllabus

Every FAQ answer should align with your official syllabus. Update both documents together to avoid contradictions.
