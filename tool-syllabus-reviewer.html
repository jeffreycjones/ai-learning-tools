What This Review Covers

The generated prompt asks the AI to evaluate your syllabus against eight dimensions commonly flagged during quality reviews at Mays. For each dimension, the AI will provide a status (strong, needs attention, or missing), specific observations, and a concrete recommendation.

This is not a substitute for the formal Mays Course Quality Review process. It is a pre-check designed to surface issues early, so you can address them before the official review cycle.

For Flex Online courses, the review prompt also checks for Canvas-specific elements referenced in the Flex Online Course Build Guidelines and Style Guide.
